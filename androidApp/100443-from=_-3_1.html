App Description

View your photos and trips in a fascinating way. Keep your memories alive like never before. PhotoMap is one of the best gallery apps with unique features. It helps you finding great places again. Relive your trips by jumping virtually from location to location.

"PhotoMap is one of the more unique gallery apps. [...] It's also just fun to play with." - Android Authority, 4/2017

"This is useful for holiday, sports and business photos." - Android Magazin, 4/2015

With PhotoMap, you can view photos and videos from your internal storage or SD card. PhotoMap also supports cloud storage and network drives: Dropbox, Google Drive, Microsoft OneDrive, FTP/FTPS, and CIFS/SMB.

If your photos don't have geo positions until now, please activate the corresponding option in your camera app. It is recommended to use the app MapCam to take photos with accurate geo positions.

More features:
✔ View your photos in a clear calendar view.
✔ Track your trips or your everyday life with the integrated geo tracker.
✔ Import GPX files from external trackers.
✔ Export your trips to GPX/KML files.
✔ View your photos in an amazing Augmented Reality (AR) view.
✔ Write down your thoughts and facts in a diary.
✔ Use the helpful homescreen widgets.
✔ Change the location of your photos via drag and drop.
✔ Add GPS positions to photos in geotagging mode.
✔ Store photo captions in your photos' metadata.
✔ View Exif, IPTC, XMP, ICC and other meta data.
✔ Enjoy preview pictures situated on a zoomable world map (photo map).
✔ Freely zoom, turn or tilt the 3D world map to enjoy different perspectives.
✔ Freely choose between satellite, street, terrain, and OpenStreetMap view.

PhotoMap is a great tool for many scenarios:
★ When traveling, on vacation, on business trips, when camping, in foreign cities, etc.
★ Make a note of sites like attractions, real estate, homes, cars, construction sites, restaurants, places of work, hotels, hostels, schools, parking lots, etc.
★ During sport such as bicycling, rowing, running, jogging, skiing, snowboarding, hiking, trekking, exercising, playing football, etc.
★ As a spectator at a special event such as a concert, a festival, a football game, a tennis match, etc.
★ As a visitor to parks, zoos, flea markets, amusement parks, Christmas markets, etc.
★ On the family outing, at a party/celebration, at a bar/nightclub, to record the night life, the weekend, etc.
★ When meeting with friends at the mall, on the playground, while shopping, in a restaurant, etc.
★ On special occasions such as parades, birthdays, weddings, celebrations, etc.
★ In the event of a disaster such as an earthquake, storm surge, flood, tsunami, storm, hurricane, forest fire, pandemics, etc.

PhotoMap is useful for many groups of people, such as real estate brokers, geologists, inspectors, rangers, craftsmen, firefighters, reporters, police officers, postmen, architects, backpackers, travelers, construction workers, truck drivers, taxi drivers, event managers, stewardesses, guest representatives, actors, facility managers, au pairs, tourism assistants, farmers, foresters, gardeners, insurance agents, janitors, pilots, doctors, interior designers, city guides, models, etc.
